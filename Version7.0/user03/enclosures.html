Enclosures


Never keep your clown in a cramped space! Clowns require a lot of space to live happy and healthy lives. A good rule of thumb is to dedicate at least 300 sq. ft. of space per clown in your home. Large, brightly colored tents can be bought from online vendors or your local carnival.

A clown tent like the one pictured to the right is far too small to be any clown's permanent home. Tents like these should only be used when either raising newly hatched clownlings, quarantining a sick clown from others, or transporting clowns over a long distance.

Make sure to clean your clowns' tent regularly! Make a habit of sweeping the stage and dusting the tarp at least once a week.
