The First Cat to Space

Félicette,

a stray cat from the animal shelter was chosen among 13 other cats to the space program. This space program was to test the safety before humans could be sent to space and gather data. Throughout 1957 to 1963, multiple procedures were done on the cats involving electrodes being implanted onto their skull.

The 13 cats had all died except Félicette though was killed and dissected for data for research afterwards.
