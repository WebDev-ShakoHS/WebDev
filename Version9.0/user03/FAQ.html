The Trickster's Cauldron

Tavern and Inn

What is the currency that you use?
We use the standard gold-silver-copper system. 10 copper pieces (cp) is worth 1 silver piece (sp), and 10 silver pieces are worth 1 gold piece (gp). If you don't have the right currency on hand, go get what you have exchanged at the treasury in the merchant's district!
What are your hours?
We are open 24 hours a day, 7 days a week! Of course we won't be serving meals in the wee hours of the morning, but you're always welcome to grab seat at the bar or rent a room.
Do you provide vegan options?
Unfortunately we do not have any vegan meals on the menu. However you are welcome to ask for any of our non-meat side-dishes at a lower price!
Is this tavern a good place to lie low and avoid any brushes with the law?
We are well aware that all sorts frequent our establishment, therefore we have a strict privacy policy. Our staff won't ask any questions so long as you don't bring any trouble. As a bonus we demand that any townsguard coming to snoop around bring a warrent!
Is it true that The Trickster's Cauldron is one of the hot spots for the Thieves' Guild?
